Koenigsegg Jesko Attack

The Koenigsegg Jesko Attack is a high-performance hypercar designed for maximum downforce and track dominance.

Specifications

  • Engine: 5.0L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Power: 1600 HP (on E85 fuel)
  • Torque: 1500 Nm
  • Top Speed: 480+ km/h
  • 0-100 km/h: Under 2.8 seconds
  • Downforce: 1400 kg at 275 km/h
